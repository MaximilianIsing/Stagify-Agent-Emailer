Parameterized bulk email

Send a custom Stagify-branded email to many recipients. Upload a CSV, write one template with {placeholders}, preview with real data, then batch send.

How it works

  1. Upload a CSV — first column is always email addresses; every other column is a parameter.
  2. Write your email — use {ColumnName} anywhere in the subject or body (names must match CSV headers).
  3. Optional images — add image URLs below, then insert {image1}, {image2}, etc. in the body.
  4. Preview — see the rendered email for a sample recipient before sending.
  5. Batch send — sends one personalized email per CSV row.

Example CSV (Example.csv)

Email,Name
bob@gmail.com,Bob Jones
akahn@outlook.com,Alex Kahn

Example subject

A note for {Name}

Example body

We are excited to show you our new update!
